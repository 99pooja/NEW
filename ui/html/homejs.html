About Us


Former Sr. Executives from Bosch, Cognizant and iNautix have teamed up to bring Arwizon Digital to fruition. We are headquartered in the silicon city of India, Bangalore and currently having a business partnership with HikeOn Inc in Texas, USA. A rapidly growing team, with experienced resources to meet the rapid growth in clientele. We cater to the Small and Medium Business (SMB) segment, who are early adopters of the cloud-based Digital Commerce solutions.

Rajesh Vedhamurthy

CEO, Arwizon

Rajesh has about 11 years of experience in IT. He was employed in the US with a multinational Inautix Technologies, working for Bank of New York when he decided to take a plunge as an entrepreneur, He was hardly 27 years of age. His passion for technology, as well as Cricket, lead him to start a fantasy cricketing app called “Crickathon”. He currently heads the entire eCommerce IT practice for clients in the USA like Restaurantware Inc, Pearson publications, Booksback.com etc.
